Our Vision

Beyond the Screen

Aether Dynamics was founded to release digital productivity from the confines of flat glass panels. We make hardware that fits naturally into the human environment.

Merging Optics & Artificial Intelligence

We believe the ultimate interface is invisible. By combining advanced holographic projections with low-power infrared gesture tracking and edge AI agents, Aether offers a seamless way to command files, notifications, and code.

Rather than scrolling through dozens of browser tabs, our spatial software arranges your active tasks in 3D space around your desk, allowing you to drag, resize, and discard windows with simple gestures.

Technical Specifications
Display Light-Field Holographic Projector, 120Hz refresh rate, 3500 nits peak brightness, 4K spatial resolution.
Sensors 2x wide-angle infrared depth cameras, 1x high-definition ambient light sensor, 1x solid-state radar (presence sensing).
Processor Octa-core Neural Processing Unit (NPU) for offline AI processing and instantaneous hand gesture mapping.
Audio Dual-driver spatial speakers with passive radiator, Dolby Atmos support, and quad beam-forming microphones.
Connectivity Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.4, USB-C (Power Delivery & DisplayPort input).
Materials CNC-machined aerospace-grade aluminum, recycled matte glass, biodegradable packaging.
Dimensions 12.4 cm x 12.4 cm x 8.6 cm (Base diameter & height).
